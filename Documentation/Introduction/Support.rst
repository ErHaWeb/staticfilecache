Need Support?
=============

There are various ways to get support for the StaticFileCache extension!

Stack Overflow
--------------

Please use `Stack Overflow <https://stackoverflow.com/search?q=%5Btypo3%5D+staticfilecache>`_
to get the best support and tag your question with `typo3`.

Slack
-----

The dedicated channel `#ext-staticfilecache <https://typo3.slack.com/messages/ext-staticfilecache/>`_
of the TYPO3 Slack Workspace can be used to get in direct contact with other
users.

If you are not registered yet, follow this
`guide <https://typo3.org/community/meet/chat-slack/>`_.

Personal support
----------------

If you need private or personal support, ask one of the developers for it.

**Be aware that this support is not be free of charge!**
